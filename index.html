Privacy Policy - Tage Master

Last updated: February 1, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Tage Master collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application. We are committed to ensuring the protection of your privacy and the security of your personal data.

Data Collection

Our app collects the following information via Apple Sign In:

  • Last name: Your surname
  • First name: Your given name
  • Email address: The email address linked to your Apple account

This data is collected only when signing in with Apple and is used to personalize your experience within the app.

Use of Data

The collected information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • App functionality
  • Account authentication
  • Personalizing your user experience
  • Essential communication related to your account

We do not collect any other types of personal data and do not use your information for other purposes.

Storage and Security

We take your data security seriously. All information is protected by the built-in security features of Apple Sign In. We do not store your data on external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. We do not use tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Log out from the app at any time
  • Modify your Apple Sign In preferences via Apple settings

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be published in the app and on this page.
